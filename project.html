Game Description

Battle against the computer for control of the sea. Win the game by:

  • Taking turns firing blindly onto the enemy's grid
  • Once all of the enemy's ships are sunk, the player wins.

Game Layout

The player will have view of their own grid with their 11 ships and view of the enemy's grid, which will be empty. The enemy grid will record where the player has fired, whether it be a hit or miss.
